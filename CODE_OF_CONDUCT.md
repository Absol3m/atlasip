# Code of Conduct

## Overview
AtlasIP is an open‑source project that aims to provide a respectful, inclusive, and collaborative environment for contributors and users.  
This Code of Conduct outlines expectations for participation and describes how to report unacceptable behavior.

All contributors, maintainers, and participants are expected to follow this Code of Conduct.

---

## Our Standards

### Positive behaviors include:
- Being respectful and considerate  
- Using inclusive and welcoming language  
- Providing constructive feedback  
- Being open to different viewpoints  
- Showing empathy toward others  
- Focusing on what is best for the community  

### Unacceptable behaviors include:
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks  
- Insults, derogatory comments, or trolling  
- Publishing private information without permission  
- Threats or violent language  
- Disruptive or intentionally harmful behavior  
- Any conduct that violates applicable laws  

---

## Responsibilities of Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:
- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior  
- Reviewing and addressing reported issues  
- Taking appropriate corrective action when necessary  
- Ensuring a safe and respectful environment  

Maintainers have the right to:
- Edit, remove, or reject comments, commits, issues, or pull requests that violate this Code of Conduct  
- Temporarily or permanently ban contributors for repeated or severe violations  

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project spaces (GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, documentation, etc.)  
- Public interactions related to the project  
- Private communications involving project maintainers or contributors  

---

## Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by contacting the maintainers.

Reports should include:
- A description of the incident  
- When and where it occurred  
- Any relevant context  
- Any supporting evidence (if available)  

All reports will be handled confidentially.

---

## Enforcement

Maintainers will:
1. Review the report  
2. Investigate the situation  
3. Take appropriate action, which may include:
   - A warning  
   - Temporary suspension  
   - Permanent ban  
   - Removal of content  

Actions will be proportional to the severity of the violation.
